Pokey Pattern/2013
Part of my larger project Pokey Product Suite Go to project page for more>>
I designed this pattern for use across a suite of products (varying from shoulder bags to sketchbook covers) to unify them all around a character. The tile for this is huge, a consequence of prioritizing keeping a consistent density, and keeping the tile's "seams" hidden. It led me to later question if I'd met my goal: maybe the seams arent obvious because they're fewer, not because they're well hidden.
