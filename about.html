Hello!

My name is Aaminah Cole, I'm a 21 year old illustration student currently in her final year at MICA.

It can probably be said for all illustrators, but I am an extremely visually oriented person—love to look and can find inspiration from the most unexpected sources.My interests are wide ranging, from Euclidian geometry to extreme electronic music and these varied influences are felt in my equally eclectic work. As my work shows, I'm confident in my ability to work in more than just illustrative capacity. If you have a special project in mind, shoot me an email and we can work out the details.
